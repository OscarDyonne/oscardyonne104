Interests

Being a Taurean, I enjoy good food and I like cooking for family and friends. I appreciate outdoor activities. For seven years now, I have been a member of my local rowing club in Olivet. During the summer, I go trekking in the Pyrenées region, where I had the privilege to do a falconry initiation. Nature is important to me. In the evenings after school, I regularly play the guitar to relax and practise new riffs.


I am a conversationalist, and enjoy meeting and learning from new people. This helped me when I was a rowing instructor during a team-building seminar hosted by my rowing club. I am curious about the world around me. I like to read about, and share technological developments, in fields such as the automotive industry and science. In my opinion there is no useless knowledge.
